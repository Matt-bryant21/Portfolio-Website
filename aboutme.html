About Me


Hello again! My name is Matt Bryant, I’m originally from Wollongong, NSW but currently residing in the Sydney CBD. I am a social creature with a love for technology and understanding of how things work.

I am predominantly a self taught jeweller running my own small business Uncelebrated Jewellery, but I am eager to shift into the web dev world as a means to create a steady career path that will allow me to grow my business organically. My creativity flourishes when I am able to create beautiful pieces of jewellery, and this translates into web development and the achieving of small goals that lead to bigger outcomes. The placement of a precious stone is comparable to writing a small line of code that has a butterfly effect on the bigger picture.

Outside of jewellery and my studies, I enjoy eating good food, staying active as much as possible, the ocean, any form of socializing, and all animals.

My personality brings strong people skills and a desire to connect with everyone around me. I am extremely empathetic and always motivated towards personal growth and development. A fast paced environment suits me best as I thrive under pressure and love nothing more than a terrifying challenge.
